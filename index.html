Inside the work:
Atlas Design System.

The Challenge

A 300-person team with 5 different visual languages.

Atlas Corp had grown rapidly through acquisitions. Each product team had their own components, tokens, and opinions. The result: a fragmented user experience that confused customers and slowed engineers maintaining dozens of duplicate components.

My mandate: unify the visual language, ship a Figma library and React component library simultaneously, and do it without disrupting active product development across three teams.

My Role

Lead Designer & Architect Â· 14 months Â· Team of 3 designers

The Approach

Audit, unify, document, ship.

Started with a full component audit across all products â€” 340+ unique components reduced to 87 canonical ones. Built a token architecture that could flex across three product families while maintaining a coherent brand voice throughout.

Partnered directly with 4 engineering leads to ensure the Figma library and React library stayed in perfect sync. Weekly syncs, an open Slack channel, and living documentation made adoption actually happen at scale.

The final system launched Q3 2023 and became the org's most-used internal resource within 6 weeks of release. Zero regressions, 100% team adoption.
